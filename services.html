Growing More Than Just Plants: Our Range of Plant-Powered Services!

Plant Identification

Our plant identification tool can help you identify the name and care instructions of a plant you have or want to buy. Simply upload a photo of your plant or describe its features, and our tool will provide you with the most likely match and care instructions.

Plant Care Advice

We offer comprehensive information on how to care for different types of plants, including watering, lighting, soil requirements, pruning, and fertilization. Our plant care guide is a valuable resource for both novice and experienced gardeners.

Online Plant Store

Our online plant store offers a wide range of plants, seeds, and gardening supplies for sale. We have a vast selection of indoor and outdoor plants, rare and exotic species, and gardening tools and accessories. Shop with us for all your plant needs!

Garden Design Services

Our garden design services can help you plan and create your dream garden. Our team of experienced designers can provide site analysis, layout design, plant selection, and maintenance plans tailored to your specific needs and preferences. Let us help you bring your garden to life!

Plant Rental

We offer a plant rental service for businesses and homes looking to add some greenery to their space without the commitment of buying and maintaining plants. We provide a wide range of indoor plants on rent with flexible rental periods and maintenance plans.

Community Platform

Our community platform is a place for plant enthusiasts to connect, share their knowledge and experiences, and learn from each other. Join our community to get inspired, ask questions, and meet fellow plant lovers!

Plant-Related Workshops and Classes

We offer a range of workshops and classes on various plant-related topics, such as how to propagate plants, plant-based DIY projects, and cooking with herbs. Our workshops and classes are designed for both novice and experienced gardeners looking to expand their knowledge and skills.

Consultancy Services

Our consultancy services provide expert advice and support to businesses, institutions, or individuals on plant-related matters. We can help with plant selection, garden design, plant maintenance, and other plant-related issues. Contact us to learn more about our consultancy services.
